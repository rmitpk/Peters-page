Description.

The software I would love to engineer is a simple platform on the user front but may be a bit more complicated back end I assume. The software will allow users their own portal, in that portal they would be able to log in from their device weather phone or computer, I feel a web portal is sufficient although an app would be beneficial, I can see people more hesitant to install apps. Hence the web portal would suffice. In this portal you will have the opportunity to link your social media, email & or phone contacts to your profile. The software will display the Netflix shows and your contacts review on those shows. There will be two avenues to read your contacts reviews. Via Netflix show & Via username. - Via Netflix show This page will list all the shows in a graph, this graph will be set to show highest rating first. This page has the ability to average the star rating from your contacts to list the Netflix shows in assumed preference according to my social circle. Example is the software computes all the ratings in my friend’s circle and averages them out into the spreadsheet. There is a toggle on/off Favorite person button on this page also. If no one has reviewed a show its at the bottom equal to not having many stars. If many of my friends like a show it is pushed up the list. Like what google does with business reviews, the greater number of positive reviews the higher the ranking. This page will be good if your unsure what to watch you can check highest ranked show and whack it on the tv. - Via username In the case you have the one mate like John above who I know I love his taste in shows I can click his name and see the list of his reviews. There will be a basic adjustable graph that lists. A, Show name B, Genre C, Date reviewed D Star rating C comments. Each column the user will be able to toggle priority of the column and there will be a search feature. The search would be active in all columns. E.g., I love sci fi so I might search sci fi then toggle the star rating set to highest rating first. That way it will show me the most relevant items to what I am wanting to watch. This page is good if you have that epic mate like John who you love his shows and trust his reviews.

Weight of friend.

In the case like my situation, you have two friends who you can/can’t trust reviews you can mark one friend as a favorite, increasing the weight of their reviews. Backend we would need a super admin portal where we can see all the data input by users, this will be useful for IT support or issues, tracking usage and selling data to advertising *permitted by user of course in T&C 😊
